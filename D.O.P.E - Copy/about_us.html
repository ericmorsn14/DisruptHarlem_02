D.O.P.E. Entertainment is a company founded by Eric Morrison.Based on the the acronym, Dopeness Onbrings Power and Envy, D.O.P.E. Ent's number one goal is to connect artists to managers. Artists have the power to create, perform and promote their works, whereas managers can create a profile and view the artists they would like to work with. Gone are the days where artists struggle to obtain management. Thats why we're here-to make the connection for you!

Eric Morrison founded D.O.P.E. based on his passion of music. In the past, he has collaborated with youth agencies for complilation albums and he has recently performed at The Whitney American Muesuem Of Art. He knows what it's like to struggle as an artist. He hopes to make it easier for upcomming artists to come up.
